strawberry collagen yakult

1. Prepare the strawberries by washing them, removing the stems, and cutting them into small cubes or fine pieces. Set them aside.
2. Prepare the collagen milk by combining milk and collagen powder in a glass. Use a spoon or a frother to mix thoroughly. Set it aside.
3. Assemble the drink by adding the strawberries and sugar to a glass. Use a muddler or the back of a spoon to mix until the strawberry juice is released and the sugar is fully dissolved.
4. Add ice cubes, collagen milk, and 2 bottles of Yakult.
5. Garnish with strawberry slices.

matcha yakult

1. Prepare your matcha shot by adding matcha powder and water into a bowl.
2. Make sure to sift the matcha powder first and use a whisk or frother to mix until there are no clumps. Set aside.
3. In a serving glass, add in your ice cubes, milk, Yakult and matcha shot.
