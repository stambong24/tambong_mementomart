yakult

Hailing from Japan, Yakult is a sweet probiotic drink, loosely comparable to Western yogurt drinks such as Danino and Yop. However, the difference lies in its thinner consistency, and distinct flavour, which is unique in and of itself. The drinks may be small, but they pack a punch, and will leave you wanting more. Today, Yakult is now sold all over the world.
