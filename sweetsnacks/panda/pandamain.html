hello panda

Hello Panda was created by the Meiji Seika company, and consists of small biscuits made of shortbread filled with chocolate. Each biscuit has a unique design, depicting pandas carrying out fun activities like skateboarding, soccer, basketball, and more. This snack is cleverly designed, as its bright and dynamic packaging immediately catches the eye of any passerby, children and adults alike. The panda shape of the shortbread is an added bonus, as it really drives home the panda theme.
