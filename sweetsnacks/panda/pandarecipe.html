homemade hello panda

original recipe here

1. Mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a bowl.
2. Pour cooled, melted butter and soy milk into dry ingredients and mix till you get a soft dough.
3. Roll dough out to a 1/4 cm thickness and use a 2 inch cookie cutter to cut out round shapes. Drop a tsp of melted chocolate onto each dough and wrap.
4. Brush with egg white and bake in a preheated oven of 150 degrees C for 35 mins.
5. paint panda onto cookies using food colouring with a toothpick and let dry.
