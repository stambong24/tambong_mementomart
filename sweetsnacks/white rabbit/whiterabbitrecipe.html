ingredients

Liquid Maltose, White Granulated, Sugar, Whole Milk Powder, Butter, Food Additives (Gelatin, Vanillin), Edible Glutinous Rice Paper (Edible Corn Starch, Edible Cassava Starch, Water, Glycerin, Monostearate)

Richard Nixon, America’s 37th president, was presented with White Rabbit candies as gifts during his visit to China. White Rabbit candy is also used as the base for many dessert spots around the world for menu items such as ice cream and bubble tea.

white rabbit ice cream

original recipe here

1. Heat some White Rabbit candy and milk on medium heat on the stove using a small pot.
2. Stir occasionally, until the candy is melted.
3. In a mixing bowl, add heavy cream, milk powder, and vanilla extract. Mix with an electric mixer to form stiff peaks.
4. Slowly pour the cooled melted white rabbit mixture into the whipped cream and fold together with a spatula until no streaks remain
5. Pour the mixture into a loaf pan or freezer-safe container. Cover and freeze for at least 6 hours or overnight.
6. When serving, leave the ice cream out at room temperature for about 10 minutes and run an ice cream scooper under hot water before scooping.

white rabbit cookies

original recipe here

1. Mix butter, sugar, and brown sugar for 2 minutes in a stand mixer on medium speed.
2. Add egg and vanilla and mix on medium speed for 2 minutes. Scrap down the sides of the bowl occasionally.
3. Mix in flour, baking soda and salt until just incorporated.
4. Stir in chocolate chunks and white rabbit candies.
5. Portion out 2 tbsp. of cookie dough and place it on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Repeat with remaining dough. Refrigerate for 30 minutes, then roll the dough in between your palms to form balls. Repeat with remaining dough. Refrigerate for 30 minutes more, for a total of one hour.
6. Preheat the oven to 350˚F. Bake the cookies for 12-15 minutes until the edges are golden brown and the center is slightly gooey. Depending on the size of your cookie sheet, you may need to bake in batches.
7. Transfer to a cooling rack and top with more chocolate chunks, white rabbit candies, and Maldon sea salt flakes if desired. Serve warm.
