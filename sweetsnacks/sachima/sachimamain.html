sachima

Sachima is a snack with a long and rich history. Its most simple form calls for a fluffy and sticky sweet treat that is easy to eat with a soft texture. However, the many versions of Sachima vary depending on the region, including different places in China, and throughout the rest of Asia as well. For those unfamiliar with the snack, a similar dessert are Rice Krispie treats, which have a similar texture. Although, the taste is significantly different, and they are definitely more than worth a try.
