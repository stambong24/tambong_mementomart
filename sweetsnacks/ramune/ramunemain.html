ramune

The development of Ramune can be traced all the way back to the 1880s, and has risen to popularity due to its unique shape, and engaging packaging. The most distinct part of a Ramune bottle is the marble, which the consumer is meant to pop back into the bottle in order to open it. If not for the soda, at least try buying a bottle for the experience, as it is one like no other with a satisfying fizz.
