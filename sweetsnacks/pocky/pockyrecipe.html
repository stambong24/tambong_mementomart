homemade pocky

1. Add flour, sugar, and salt into a food processor and pulse on or two times.
2. Add the chopped butter into the food processor and pulse two or three times until it resembles coarse bread crumbs.
3. Add the milk and pulse again.
4. Wrap the ball of dough with saran wrap/cling film and leave it in the fridge for 30 minutes.
5. Preheat the oven to 180 degrees Celsius (or 350 degrees Fahrenheit).
6. Roll the dough out until it’s about 5mm thick.
7. Form it into a rectangle shape, cutting off the excess dough on the sides. Then cut it into thin pieces about 5mm (or 0.19 inches) wide.
8. Place the sticks in the oven and bake for 15 minutes.
9. Coat with melted chocolate, and any other toppings of your choice.

pocky cookie lolipops

original recipe here

1. Start by whisking flour, baking soda, and baking powder together in a medium-sized bowl.
2. In a large bowl, combine wet ingredients using a mixer. Whip together sugar and butter until well combined and fluffy. Then, add egg and vanilla extract, mixing till smooth.
3. Once wet ingredients are combined, add dry ingredients, in batches, until fully incorporated. Then, roll out dough and use cookie cutters to make shapes. Place shapes on a parchment-lined cookie sheet and bake at 375 degrees for 8 to 10 min or until the sides of cookies are lightly golden.
4. Take them out of the oven and use the biscuit side of a Pocky stick to make indents onto the warm cookies. Then, let them sit for about 5 minutes before transferring to a cooling rack.
5. Mix melted white chocolate with food coloring until you achieve desired color.
6. Dip the frosted side of a Pocky stick in the melted chocolate and then place in the indent in the cooled cookie and place on a wax-paper-lined tray. Place the tray in the freezer for 5 min to set.
7. Take them out of the freezer and frost the cookies with melted white chocolate.
