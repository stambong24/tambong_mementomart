pocky

The original versions of Pocky are simple, but delicious, consisting of a biscuit stick covered in chocolate. The name “pocky”, actually comes from the Japanese onomatopoeia, “pokkiri”, in reference to the crunch sound of the biscuit. The stick shape of the snack makes it perfect to share with friends, and share one’s love for the iconic biscuit. It's also just super easy to eat.
