about memento mart

I intended on making Memento Mart a website that people can relate to. As such, it presented me with an opportunity to seek out opinions about these snacks outside of my own. That’s where my friends, family, and peers came in! I received a lot of valuable input from them, including what snacks were most popular among them, and what would leave the most impact. I also wanted to make sure that their ideas were evident on the website itself, which is why I included the little thought bubbles for each history section. I wanted this website to be fun for the user, and the most important part in achieving that was making sure that the development wasn’t a solo venture. Thus, Memento Mart is an archive that exists because of the memories and passion of many people!
