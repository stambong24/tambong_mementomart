buldak

In recent years, Buldak noodles, also known as fire noodles, have risen to popularity thanks to social media. These instant noodles are unique, as they are extremely spicy to the point where people around the world have participated in the “Buldak Fire Noodle Challenge”, in which the goal is to finish a single serving. However, these instant noodles are not only popular for their novelty, but for their taste too.
