rice crackers

Asian rice crackers come in many shapes and sizes, and many of which have a delicious umami flavour to them. These are light, airy, and perfect for a quick snack — especially if you’re craving a satisfying crunch. Rice crackers also come in a plethora of different flavours, and some are even spicy if you prefer that! Regardless, rice crackers are the type of snack with something for everyone, as its base is a simple blank canvas for a variety of different seasonings.
